# Privacy Policy for Goaly

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026

Welcome to Goaly. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Our privacy policy is very simple and transparent.

## 1. No Data Storage
We do not store, retain, or share any of your personal data. 

## 2. No Analytics or Tracking
Goaly does not use any analytics, third-party tracking, or behavior monitoring tools. Your use of the application is completely private.

## 3. How We Handle Your Session and Calendar
To provide our service, we only perform the following actions:
- **Session Management:** We maintain your active session so you can stay logged in.
- **Calendar Integration:** We act strictly on your behalf to set and manage goal-related events directly on your personal calendar (e.g., Google Calendar).

We do not copy or save your calendar events to any external database. All calendar data remains securely between your device and your calendar provider.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us on Github.
